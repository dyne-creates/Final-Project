About Us

Imagine Dragons is a Grammy Award-winning American alternative pop rock band. Formed in 2008 and based in Las Vegas, Nevada, the group has become known globally for their anthemic sound. The band's name is actually an anagram of a phrase known only to the members, and their devoted fanbase affectionately refers to them as the "fire breathers."
