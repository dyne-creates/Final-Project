Meet our Members

Dan Reynolds

Daniel Coulter Reynolds (born July 14, 1987) is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is the lead singer and only remaining original member of the pop rock band Imagine Dragons.

Ben McKee

Ben "Soft Hands" McKee (born April 7, 1985) is an American musician, songwriter, and record producer. He is the bassist for Imagine Dragons.

Daniel Wayne Sermon

Daniel Wayne Sermon (born June 15, 1984) is an Americal musician, songwriter, record producer, and the lead guitarist in Imagine Dragons.
